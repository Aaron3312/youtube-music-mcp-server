Search YouTube Music for songs, artists, albums, and playlists. Generate mood- or decade-based suggestions and create smart playlists from natural language descriptions. Authenticate to create and manage playlists in your own library.

## Quick Setup (2 minutes)

1. Go to music.youtube.com and sign in
2. Press F12 → Console tab → Run: copy(document.cookie)
3. Paste the result in youtube_music_cookies field below

## Features
• Search music without auth
• Create/manage playlists (requires cookies)
• Generate smart playlists from descriptions
• Add/remove songs from playlists

## Example Commands
"Search for Beatles songs"
"Create a workout playlist"
"Add this song to my favorites"
"Generate a chill study playlist with 30 songs"

Cookies last ~2 weeks. Details: github.com/CaullenOmdahl/youtube-music-mcp-server
